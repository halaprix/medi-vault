# ADA Diabetes Guidelines

## Fasting Glucose
- Normal: < 100 mg/dL
- Prediabetes: 100-125 mg/dL
- Diabetes: ≥ 126 mg/dL

## HbA1c
- Normal: < 5.7%
- Prediabetes: 5.7-6.4%
- Diabetes: ≥ 6.5%

## OGTT (2h)
- Normal: < 140 mg/dL
- Prediabetes: 140-199 mg/dL
- Diabetes: ≥ 200 mg/dL

*Source: American Diabetes Association (ADA) Standards of Care*
