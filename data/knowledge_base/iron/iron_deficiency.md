# Iron Deficiency Guidelines

## Serum Ferritin
- Deficient: < 15 ng/mL (absolute)
- Low: 15-30 ng/mL
- Normal: 30-400 ng/mL (men), 15-150 ng/mL (women)

## Serum Iron
- Normal: 60-170 mcg/dL

## TIBC
- Normal: 240-450 mcg/dL
- Elevated in iron deficiency

## Transferrin Saturation
- Normal: 20-50%
- Deficient: < 20%

*Source: WHO & Clinical Practice Guidelines*
