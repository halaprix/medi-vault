# Anti-Inflammatory Approaches

## hs-CRP
- Low risk: < 1.0 mg/L
- Average risk: 1.0-3.0 mg/L
- High risk: > 3.0 mg/L

## ESR
- Normal: 0-20 mm/hr (men), 0-30 mm/hr (women)

## Dietary Approaches
- Mediterranean diet
- Omega-3 fatty acids (1-2g/day EPA+DHA)
- Turmeric/curcumin (500-1000mg/day)

*Source: Clinical guidelines compilation*
