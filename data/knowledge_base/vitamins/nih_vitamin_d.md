# NIH Vitamin D Guidelines

## Serum 25(OH)D
- Deficient: < 20 ng/mL (50 nmol/L)
- Insufficient: 20-29 ng/mL (50-74 nmol/L)
- Sufficient: 30-100 ng/mL (75-250 nmol/L)
- Potential toxicity: > 150 ng/mL (375 nmol/L)

## Recommendations
- Adults 19-70: 600 IU/day (15 mcg)
- Adults >70: 800 IU/day (20 mcg)

*Source: NIH Office of Dietary Supplements*
