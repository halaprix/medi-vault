# AHA Cholesterol Guidelines

## Total Cholesterol
- Desirable: < 200 mg/dL
- Borderline: 200-239 mg/dL
- High: ≥ 240 mg/dL

## LDL Cholesterol
- Optimal: < 100 mg/dL
- Near Optimal: 100-129 mg/dL
- Borderline: 130-159 mg/dL
- High: 160-189 mg/dL
- Very High: ≥ 190 mg/dL

## HDL Cholesterol
- Low (risk): < 40 mg/dL (men), < 50 mg/dL (women)
- Optimal: ≥ 60 mg/dL

## Triglycerides
- Normal: < 150 mg/dL
- Borderline: 150-199 mg/dL
- High: 200-499 mg/dL
- Very High: ≥ 500 mg/dL

*Source: American Heart Association (AHA) 2018 Guidelines*
