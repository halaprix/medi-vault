# Privacy Policy

## Data Storage

All your health data is stored **locally** on your machine. Nothing is sent to external servers.

## Encryption

- **At rest**: All sensitive data (health metrics, OAuth tokens) is encrypted using Fernet (AES-128-CBC + HMAC)
- **In transit**: HTTPS (self-signed in development)

## What We Collect

- Medical test results you upload
- Biomarker values extracted from documents
- Google Fit data (if you choose to connect)
- PIN hash (for local authentication)

## What We Do NOT Collect

- No telemetry
- No analytics
- No cloud sync
- No third-party sharing

## Data Deletion

Delete individual documents or use "Delete All Data" in Settings.
