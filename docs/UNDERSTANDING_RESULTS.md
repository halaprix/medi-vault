# Understanding Your Results

## Reference Ranges

Each biomarker has a reference range (normal values). Results outside this range are flagged.

### Color Coding
- 🟢 **In range**: Value within normal limits
- 🟡 **Borderline**: Slightly outside range
- 🔴 **Out of range**: Significantly outside — consult your doctor

## Trends

The **Trend** view shows how each biomarker changes over time. Look for:
- **Upward trends**: May indicate improvement or worsening (depends on biomarker)
- **Downward trends**: Same — context matters
- **Sudden changes**: Could indicate lab error or real health changes

## Recommendations

AI-generated recommendations are based on medical guidelines (AHA, ADA, NIH, ATA). They are **advisory only** — always consult your healthcare provider.
